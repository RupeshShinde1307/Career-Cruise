About Infosys

Infosys is a renowned global leader in consulting, technology services, and digital transformation solutions. Operating across numerous countries and employing a workforce of over 250,000 professionals worldwide, Infosys is dedicated to empowering businesses with cutting-edge technology to enhance innovation and realize their objectives. The company provides a comprehensive suite of services spanning strategy, consulting, digital, technology, and operations. Through its unwavering commitment to delivering exceptional value to clients and promoting a culture of inclusivity and collaboration, Infosys remains steadfast in its mission to spearhead positive transformations in the digital landscape.
